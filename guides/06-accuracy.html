Accuracy

Below are results of the 5-fold cross validation comparisons for MYOP, Augutus and SNAP. The boxes exhibit the average values and standard deviation across all 5 runs for each organism. We measured whole-gene, whole-exon and nucleotide sensitivity (SN), positive predictive value (PPV) and F-score (F). Due to high variance between runs of the 5-fold cross validation, we applied the Wilcoxon’s paired hypothesis test for each pair of values. Numbers in green indicate measures where MYOP was had a better performance with p-value less then 0.05. Number in red indicate measures where either SNAP or Augutus had a better performance with p-value less than 0.05 when compared to MYOP.
